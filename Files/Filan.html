Filan Tech

Filan Tech is a technology institute that invests in the younger generation of tomorrow's leaders and promotes skills that do not require a degree. Every student can benefit from it in a short period of time. There is a great need for this field for students from IT backgrounds as well as those from non-IT backgrounds. Our team is interested in creating digital products for the learned students who want to study these programs. FilanTech is an education and work site where one's job is to teach oneself the skills of programming and English. The purpose of FilanTech is to provide job training to the person while learning the skill and, after learning, to help them find a job. Because Filantech is about developing skills that can make people work for themselves.

Executive Summary

Our company is interested in investigating potential in programming-learning websites because our product is still in its early stages. Five learners with and without IT backgrounds were questioned about their needs, pain points, and preferred methods of learning this program.
